About me

After receiving my B.A. in Linguistics and Human-Computer Interaction at Carnegie Mellon University, I decided to pursue HCI further at UMD. Throughout my studies, I have pursued different areas of UI/UX design and development: user research, responsive website design, dashboard design, and front-end web development.
My background in linguistics drives my interest in understanding the ways language and technology interact and form greater digital experiences, while my background in art and animation inspires me to add moments of delight in my designs.
