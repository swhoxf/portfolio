About me

My name is Soph Ho, and I'm passionate about using design and technology to empower and inspire people. I love uncovering the nuances of how people interact with the world, learning new skills, and building worlds through art, design, and code.

After receiving my B.A. in Linguistics and Human-Computer Interaction at Carnegie Mellon University, I decided to pursue my M.S. in HCI at University of Maryland, College Park. Throughout my studies, I have pursued many aspects of UX: UX research, UX design, UX strategy, and full-stake web development. Within the field of UX, I am particularly interested in accessibility, cybersecurity, and personal health informatics. I am a detailed-oriented thinker, and I enjoy the process of working on a project from end to end and seeing how every step of the process sets the foundation for further development.
